Welcome to the SCP Foundation Containment Archive

Classified Access Only

The SCP Foundation is dedicated to the identification, containment, and study of anomalies that defy natural law. Our mission is to secure, contain, and protect humanity from phenomena that threaten normalcy. Through decades of research, field operations, and secure archival, the Foundation has developed an extensive catalog of classified objects, entities, locations, and events.

However, as our digital infrastructure has evolved, the limitations of our original web-based catalog system—first implemented in the 1990s—have become apparent. Outdated architecture, inconsistent formatting, and a lack of mobile adaptability have hindered field agents and research personnel in their crucial work.

To address these issues, the Foundation has deployed a modernized, mobile-friendly, and security-enhanced iteration of the SCP Containment Archive. This new system ensures:

Optimized Access: A responsive, mobile-adaptive design for seamless field operations.

Streamlined Updates: An improved interface allowing real-time updates and secure data entry.

Enhanced Security: Next-generation encryption and authentication protocols to maintain strict information control.

Intelligent Search & Filtering: Advanced search capabilities to provide agents with rapid, precise access to critical data.

This system remains a vital tool in our ongoing mission to protect humanity from the unknown. Only authorized personnel may proceed beyond this page.

Access requires Level 2 or higher security clearance. Unauthorized access attempts will be logged and investigated.
